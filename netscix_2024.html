Data Driven Drug Repurposing using Graph Neural Networks

Project overview

Electronic Health Records in Denmark

The BigTempHealth (BTH) project contains healthcare data from the Capital Region and Region Zealand of Denmark during the years 2006-2016 and includes ~2.9 million patients. The complete EHRs contain data on diagnoses, (hospital) prescriptions/medication, lab-test values and clinical notes.

In the current stage of this project, data on diagnoses and medication were extracted for the patients in BTH. Diagnoses assigned within the same hospital visit or within a time-span of XXX days were paired together as a diagnosis-pair. The same process was repeated for medication. Diagnoses and prescriptions given within a span of XXX days were used to generated diagnoses-prescription pairs.
